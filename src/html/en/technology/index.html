STP / DMA Execution
Fair and Transparent Dealing
Our trade execution process is simple and straight forward. The prices obtained by our liquidity providers are 100% transparent and can be disclosed to you at anytime.
1. We receive your order.
2. Our systems are constantly searching for the best price from all our liquidity providers. We match your order with the best possible price available at this instant.
3. Our system automatically applies the markup (1 pip for Standard, 0 for Premium) and executes your order. Only a few milliseconds have passed since the order has been received.
Equinix NY4 Data Center
Unparalleled Speed
Our servers are located in the Equinix NY4 data center. Equinix NY4 is a state of the art data center interconnecting the financial services industry to top financial markets worldwide. Equinix is home to the largest collection of exchanges and trading platforms.
If you use our VPS, your trades can execute with less than 1 millisecond of latency. Traders utilizing automated and high frequency trading systems can benefit from our ultra-low latency system to receive the best possible performance from their strategies.
