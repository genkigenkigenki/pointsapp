6/9/2016
UK Referendum Vote Impact: Temporary Margin Levels Update

Due to the upcoming UK vote regarding their future within the EU, we expect heavy volatility within the market. Therefore, a temporary increase in margin requirements will be in effect beginning June 16th at 20:00 GMT+1 on several FX currency crosses and CFD markets.
 
Unless notified to the contrary, on June 27th the revised margin requirements will be returned to their normal percentage levels between 20:00-22:00 GMT+1 as the markets stabilize.
 
The revised margin requirements are as follows:

GBP 7%
EUR 2%
AUD 2%

XAU 5%

 Additional increases or restrictions affecting these markets or others may follow. We will strive to inform you of any additional changes as soon as reasonably practicable.
 
Required Margin Calculations
 
NEW required margin for the symbols listed in the tables will be calculated according to the formula:
 
( Lots * Contract Size ) * ( Percent / 100 )
 
* Percent in this formula will be defined in the table below for each affected symbol.
As such, it is the percent value - not account leverage - that will determine the required margin.
 
Example: USD account with 100:1 leverage trading 1.0 lot (i.e. 100,000 units) on USDJPY with the JPY’s new 2% margin requirement would have its required margin calculated as:
(1.0 * 100,000) * (2 / 100) = 2,000 USD required margin
 
CURRENT required margin
for other symbols is calculated according to the formula:
( Lots * Contract Size / Leverage ) * ( Percent / 100 )
 
* Percent in this formula is always 100 so that account leverage alone determines the required margin.
 
Example:
USD account with 100:1 leverage trading 1.0 lot (i.e. 100,000 units) on USDCAD has its required margin calculated as:
(1.0 * 100,000 / 100) * (100 / 100) = 1,000 USD required margin
 
How does this affect me?
 
Positions in your trading account that are remaining open on an affected symbol when its margin requirement is increased will immediately require additional margin to be reserved to meet the new margin requirement in full. Pending orders that are remaining active will be subject to the new margin requirements at the time of order execution.
 
If the equity in your trading account is insufficient to cover the total required margin for all open positions, in accordance with our margin and stop out policies your open positions will automatically and without warning be closed one-by-one starting with the largest losing position, then the next largest losing position, and so on until the equity in your account is sufficient to maintain the open positions remaining.
 
Please be sure to close any open positions and remove any active orders prior to 20:00 GMT+1 on June 16th that you do not want subjected to the increased margin requirements.
 
We apologize for any inconvenience this may cause.
 
Please note, this is likely to be a period of unprecedented volatility across all currency and asset markets. Please adjust your trading style accordingly during this time.
